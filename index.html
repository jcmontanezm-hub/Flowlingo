How to Play
Everything you need to know before your first activity
🎲
Random Activities
Each level gives you a random activity. No two games are ever the same!
⏱
Beat the Clock
Every level has a time limit. Finish fast for a speed bonus on top of your score.
🏅
Score Points
Points come from correct answers plus how much time you have left. Accuracy + speed = big score!
🔁
Retry or Skip
If time runs out, retry the activity or skip it and move on. No shame either way!

📊
Final Grade
After all 6 levels, get your rank from D to S and a full score breakdown.
